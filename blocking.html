Blocking/Guarding

Like in every Street Fighter, you can hold back to block high attacks and hold down-back to block low attacks. If you block a special move or a super art, you will take a small percentage of that move's damage on block as "chip damage." You can be knocked out by chip damage, although parries negate this.

Blocking Ken's close heavy punch(on the left) won't deal any chip damage, but blocking his heavy shoryuken(on the right) will.
