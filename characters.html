Characters

From top to bottom, left to right, 3rd Strike has the following characters:

  • Akuma
  • Urien
  • Necro
  • Ibuki
  • Sean
  • Alex
  • Yun
  • Remy
  • Q
  • Chun Li
  • Makoto
  • Twelve
  • Yang
  • Ryu
  • Oro
  • Dudley
  • Elena
  • Hugo
  • Ken

Each character has a fully fleshed out moveset with normals(punches and kicks), specials(motion inputs), a throw, a leap attack, 3 selectable super arts, and a taunt.

No one character plays like another, and each fills their own special niche in the cast.
