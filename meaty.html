Meaties

To hit an attack meaty, it must make contact with the opponent on the last few frames during which it can hit. Doing so can enable more combo opportunites or more options for continuing pressure on block, as you can move quicker than normal after the move relative to the opponent. The most common ways to hit an attack meaty are to time it as the opponent is getting up or if it travels a significant distance it can be spaced properly to hit meaty.

For example, Ryu's forward medium punch typically does two hits, and you cannot combo after it. However, if done meaty on an opponent's wakeup...

You can combo after it!
