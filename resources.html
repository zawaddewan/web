Resources

Here is a list of resources that will be sure to assist you if you ever decide to pick up the game.

  • The Shoryuken Wiki - General Information about characters and mechanics including frame-data and strategies.
  • Game Restaurant - Japanese Website with very in-depth and extensive information about the game's mechanics and characters.
  • Baston - A website with some guides and a frame by frame hitbox and hurtbox viewer for move animations.
  • TheShend - A 3rd Strike archivist, has many videos on japanese 3rd strike events throughout its history.
  • 3rd Strike Youtube - A Youtube channel with many videos of the Japanese 3rd Strike Scene.
  • Sf3lp - Youtube Channel of Nica K.O., a 3rd Strike player from New York and a top 5 American player. Contains many useful tutorials for every character in the game.
  • 3rd Strike Resources SpreadsheetA spreadsheet, made by the player Artour, containing many more resources.
