Capcom has released many fighting games during their 42 years of existence, 26 of which are main line Street Fighter games. Of these games, only one is considered to be one of the greatest fighting games of all time: Street Fighter III: 3rd Strike.

This website mainly goes over the characters and system mechanics of the game.
